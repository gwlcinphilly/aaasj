Our Mission

Established in January 2019, the Asian American Alliance in South Jersey (AAASJ) is a non-profit organization dedicated to representing Asian American communities in our region.

Through our organized activities and community engagement, we strive to promote unity among Asian Americans, protect our collective interests, and increase our voice and visibility in South Jersey.

We believe in building bridges, fostering understanding, and creating opportunities for our community to thrive together.
